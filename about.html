About Crypto Intelligence

Crypto Intelligence is a free, real-time cryptocurrency dashboard designed to help investors and enthusiasts track market trends, monitor prices, and stay informed — all in one place.

What We Offer

📊

Real-Time Market Data

Live prices, 24h changes, and 7-day sparkline charts for the top 100 cryptocurrencies by market cap.

🌍

Global Market Overview

Total market capitalization, 24h trading volume, Bitcoin dominance, and active coin counts updated every minute.

😨

Fear & Greed Index

Real-time market sentiment indicator to help gauge the emotional state of the crypto market.

💼

Portfolio Tracker

Track your holdings and monitor your total portfolio value in USD or KRW — stored privately in your browser.

📰

Live News Feed

Latest cryptocurrency headlines from leading industry publications, updated automatically.

📈

Interactive Charts

Click any coin to open a full-featured TradingView chart with technical analysis tools.

Our Mission

The cryptocurrency market moves fast. Crypto Intelligence was built to give every investor — from beginner to experienced — access to the same quality of real-time data and tools that professionals use, completely free of charge.

We believe that informed investors make better decisions. By aggregating market data, news, and sentiment indicators into a single, clean interface, we aim to reduce the noise and help users focus on what matters.

Data Sources

All data displayed on Crypto Intelligence is sourced from reliable, industry-recognized third-party providers:

CoinGecko API Alternative.me (Fear & Greed) TradingView Charts CoinTelegraph News

Market data is refreshed every 60 seconds. News headlines are updated every 5 minutes.

Supported Languages & Currencies

Crypto Intelligence supports English and Korean (한국어) interface languages, and displays prices in both USD and KRW to serve a global audience.

Privacy & Your Data

We take your privacy seriously. Portfolio data and personal preferences are stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage and are never transmitted to any server. We do not require registration or collect personal data. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
